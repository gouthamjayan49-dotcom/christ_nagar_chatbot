ABOUT THE COLLEGE
-----------------
- Full Name   : Christ Nagar College (CNC)
- Location    : Maranalloor, Koovalassery P.O, Trivandrum - 695512, Kerala
- Founded     : 2012, by CMI Fathers (Carmelites of Mary Immaculate)
- Affiliated  : University of Kerala
- Status      : Unaided (Self-Financing), Rural
- Campus      : 37.5 acres, 12 km from Trivandrum city
- Students    : 1400+ enrolled
- Programmes  : 14 total (10 UG + 4 PG)
- Rankings    : 50+ National Ranks
- Add-on      : 20+ certification courses
- Website     : cnc.ac.in
- Group       : Part of Christ Nagar Group of Institutions

Motto   : "The Way, The Truth, The Life"
Vision  : To be a national leader in transforming lives through excellent,
          innovative, and compassionate education.
Mission : To inspire, prepare, and empower students to succeed in a changing world.
